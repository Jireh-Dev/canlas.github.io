Our Story

Built by Car Enthusiasts
for Car Enthusiasts

EMPWR Auto Parts and Accessories was born from a deep passion for cars — particularly the iconic Honda Civic FD (2006–2011). What started as a humble venture in Sto. Tomas, Pampanga has grown into a trusted name for car owners and enthusiasts across the region.

Our founders, car enthusiasts themselves, recognized the growing demand for quality, affordable, and genuine auto parts in Pampanga — especially for the beloved Civic FD, a car known for its reliability and tuning potential. EMPWR was established to fill that need.

Today, we serve thousands of customers — from daily drivers to seasoned car builders — providing OEM-grade parts, accessories, and reliable mechanical services with the dedication and care that only true enthusiasts can offer.
